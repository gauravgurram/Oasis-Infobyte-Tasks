Biography

Robin McLaurin Williams was an American actor and comedian. After rising to fame as Mork in the sitcom Mork & Mindy(1978–82), he went on to establish a career in both stand-up comedy and feature film acting.Thrice nominated for the coveted Academy Awards, he won it once for his much acclaimed performance in the movie ‘Good Will Hunting’. As his comedy goes, he was best known for his improvisational skills. Starting as a stand-up comedian in San Francisco and Los Angeles in the mid-1970s, he is credited with leading San Francisco’s comedy renaissance.Thus, he entered the world of movies and became the funnyman everyone fell in love with.
